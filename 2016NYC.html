2016 NYC Gaia Sprint

This page preserves the history from the 2016 NYC Gaia Sprint.

Results

Before and during the meeting, we kept jointly edited 2016 NYC Gaia Sprint Running Notes that gathered information about homework solutions, intended projects, project progress, and breakouts.

On the last day, the wrap-up was given from a jointly edited set of 2016 NYC Gaia Sprint Wrap-up Slides, which incredibly telegraphically summarize the accomplishments of the participants.

Schedule and Location

Each day begins at 08:30 NYC time and ends before 18:00 NYC time. Each day's detailed schedule will be presented and discussed each morning at 09:00.

Each day begins at 915 Broadway, 17th Floor in the space of Math for America. We will be in the Alpha and Beta rooms of Math for America. A light breakfast will be provided, lunch will be provided on four of the five days, and there will be other refreshments and surprises.

Participants should be ready to introduce themselves, their skills, code, and data, and their projects on the first day. There will also be progress reports and discussions throughout the week.
