Objectives

The idea behind the Sprints is to bring together people who have an interest in timely exploitation of the Gaia First Data Release. They are intended to facilitate work towards first papers: These are meetings of doers. The meetings will be structured to support collaborative refinement and execution of (fairly) mature scientific ideas. It is hoped that new partnerships will form at the meetings and lead to co-authored publications for the scientific literature ready or near-ready by the end of each Sprint.

Application and Registraton

Attendance will be limited. Participant selection will be based on project descriptions submitted as part of an application form. We hope to obtain a balance of scientific subjects, technical abilities, and demographics.

Watch this space for application instructions.

Travel and Lodging

Participants will be expected to make their own travel and lodging arrangements.

The Sprints do not have financial support to help with travel, but let the organizers know if there is absolutely no way you could participate without support and we can help explore options.
